Frequently Asked Questions

How long does each appointment take?
Approximately 5 minutes per person, including consent verification, vaccination, and brief observation period.
Do you provide all clinical equipment?
Absolutely. We supply vaccines, PPE, clinical consumables, and handle all sharps management and clinical waste disposal.
Can you cover multiple sites in one day?
Yes, we specialize in multi-site route planning to efficiently cover depots, offices, and facilities within a single coordinated schedule.
What reporting do you provide?
You'll receive comprehensive uptake percentages, DNA (Did Not Attend) analysis, and departmental breakdowns within 48 hours of clinic completion.
